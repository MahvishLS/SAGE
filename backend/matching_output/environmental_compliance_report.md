### Gap Analysis

The partially covered regulations pose potential risks to the company's reputation and compliance standing. The gaps in coverage are primarily related to:

* Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) plan (BRSR_env_0015)
* Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) boundaries (BRSR_env_0021)
* Energy intensity ratio (BRSR_env_0036)
* Air emissions disclosure (BRSR_env_0054, BRSR_env_0055)

These gaps may indicate a lack of detailed information or inconsistent reporting, which could lead to regulatory non-compliance, reputational damage, or stakeholder concerns.

### Specific Recommendations

To improve environmental ESG compliance and address the gaps in coverage, we recommend the following actionable steps, prioritized by impact:

1. **Develop a comprehensive EPR plan**: Establish a clear Extended Producer Responsibility plan to ensure environmentally sound management of products throughout their life cycle.
2. **Conduct a thorough Life Cycle Assessment**: Define the boundaries of the LCA and provide detailed information on the assessment to ensure transparency and compliance.
3. **Enhance energy intensity ratio reporting**: Provide a clear energy intensity ratio, based on metrics such as units of product, production volume, or number of full-time employees, to demonstrate the company's commitment to energy efficiency.
4. **Improve air emissions disclosure**: Provide contextual information and methodologies used to compile air emissions data, ensuring transparency and compliance with regulatory requirements.
5. **Regularly review and update disclosures**: Ensure that disclosures are regularly reviewed and updated to reflect changes in regulations, company operations, and stakeholder expectations.
6. **Provide training and resources**: Offer training and resources to employees responsible for ESG reporting to ensure they are equipped to provide high-quality disclosures that meet regulatory requirements.
7. **Engage with stakeholders**: Foster open communication with stakeholders, including investors, customers, and regulatory bodies, to ensure that the company's environmental compliance efforts are transparent and responsive to their concerns.

By implementing these recommendations, the company can strengthen its environmental ESG compliance, mitigate potential risks, and demonstrate its commitment to sustainability and transparency.
